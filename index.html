A Hub of Learning Excellence

PSK HI-TECH Academy is a premier coeducational private school located in Paruvachi, Tamil Nadu, dedicated to providing quality education from Kindergarten through Grade Eight. Affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), the academy emphasizes a holistic approach to learning, fostering both academic excellence and personal growth.

Roots to grow, Wings to fly

At PSK HI-TECH Academy, we are committed to nurturing young minds through a rigorous curriculum that encourages critical thinking and creativity. Our experienced faculty employs innovative teaching methodologies to ensure that each student reaches their full potential.
